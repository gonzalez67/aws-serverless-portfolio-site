Work bits

I am an Application Support Analyst and Jira Administrator with 10+ years of experience supporting enterprise applications across the private sector, public sector, and education. In my last role, I managed an Atlassian Cloud (Jira & Confluence) environment for 900+ users, acting as the go-to person for Jira administration, access management, workflow configuration, and troubleshooting.

These were some of the activities I performed:

  • Jira administration (boards, workflows, permissions, configurations).
  • Translating business requirements into effective Jira solutions.
  • Delivering regular configuration changes and continuous improvements.
  • Integrating Jira with external services (including Power BI via API).
  • Supporting users through major platform changes, including domain migrations.

In previous roles, I specialised in application support, service delivery, and release coordination, focusing on:

  • Incident and problem fixing, and change management (ITIL-aligned).
  • Coordinating developers, testers and business stakeholders.
  • Owning and coordinating the release processes to ensure smooth deployments. This activity included backlog grooming, and tracking progress.
  • Creating and maintaining extensive support and knowledge documentation.

What I enjoy the most is bringing people together to solve problems. I am a warm person who enjoys talking to people and I do believe that issues are resolved faster when we talk rather than relying on multiple teams’ messages. I’d rather talk, understand the problem properly, and fix it. In addition, I enjoy learning new technologies; one of the great things of working in IT is that you have to continue learning.
